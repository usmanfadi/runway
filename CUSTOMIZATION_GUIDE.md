# Site Customization Guide

## Aapko Ye Details Dene Hain:

### 1. Contact Information
- **Phone Number**: (format: +92 XXX XXXXXXX ya jo aap chahte hain)
- **Email Address**: (aapka business email)
- **Physical Address**: (aapka office address)
- **WhatsApp Number** (optional): (agar WhatsApp button chahiye)

### 2. Social Media Links
- **Facebook**: (Facebook page/profile URL)
- **Instagram**: (Instagram profile URL)
- **LinkedIn**: (LinkedIn company page URL)
- **Twitter/X**: (Twitter profile URL)
- **YouTube**: (YouTube channel URL)
- **TikTok**: (TikTok profile URL - agar hai)

### 3. Business Information
- **Company Name**: (aapka business name)
- **Tagline/Slogan**: (short description)
- **About Us Text**: (company ke baare mein description)

### 4. Call-to-Action Buttons
- **Primary Button Text**: (jaise "Contact Us", "Get Quote", etc.)
- **Primary Button Link**: (kahan redirect karna hai)
- **Secondary Button Text**: (agar doosra button chahiye)
- **Secondary Button Link**: (kahan redirect karna hai)

### 5. Services (Agar Different Hain)
- **Service 1 Name**: 
- **Service 1 Description**:
- **Service 2 Name**:
- **Service 2 Description**:
- (Agar aapke services different hain to batao)

### 6. Logo & Branding
- **Logo File**: (agar aapka apna logo hai)
- **Favicon**: (browser tab icon)
- **Brand Colors**: (primary color, secondary color - hex codes)

### 7. Contact Form (Agar Chahiye)
- **Form Fields**: (name, email, phone, message, etc.)
- **Form Submission**: (kahan send karna hai - email ya API)

---

## Kahan Add Honge:

1. **Header/Footer**: Phone, email, address, social links
2. **Contact Page**: Complete contact form aur details
3. **Homepage**: Call-to-action buttons, phone number
4. **About Page**: Company information, team details
5. **Service Pages**: Service descriptions (agar different hain)

---

## Example Format:

```
Phone: +92 300 1234567
Email: info@yourcompany.com
Address: Your Address, City, Country
WhatsApp: +92 300 1234567

Facebook: https://facebook.com/yourpage

LinkedIn: https://linkedin.com/company/yourcompany

Company Name: Your Company Name
Tagline: Your Business Tagline

Primary Button: "Get Started" -> /contact-us
Secondary Button: "Learn More" -> /about
```
